NTW✝️'s Creator

Learn about the person behind Navigate The Way ✝️ and the vision that drives the project.

Ted Hallum created Navigate The Way ✝️ to serve Christians who want biblically faithful help with Scripture, theology, discipleship, and ministry preparation.

Quick answer: NTW✝️ grew from practical ministry and discipleship needs: communicating God’s truth clearly, building with transparency, and keeping AI under conservative, Reformed evangelical guardrails rather than treating it as a neutral spiritual authority.

Why Ted Built NTW✝️

For biblical clarity

NTW✝️ is designed to support careful theological explanation, Bible study, and redemptive-historical insight.

For transparent AI

The project publishes its system prompt and guardrails so users can inspect the theological framework shaping answers.

For practical discipleship

The tool aims to help church leaders, lay leaders, parents, teachers, and believers prepare faithful questions and resources.

Vocational Background

Ministry analytics

Ted serves as Director of Analytics & Information Management at Ligonier Ministries, supporting global discipleship through data and information systems.

AI and leadership

His background spans military intelligence, analytics leadership, IBM oLabs, and teaching business analytics at William & Mary.

Education and service

Ted is a U.S. Army veteran and two-time Bronze Star recipient with degrees in Biblical Studies from Toccoa Falls College and Business Analytics from William & Mary.

Creator Transparency Serves Discernment

Knowing the creator and design commitments should help users evaluate the tool honestly. NTW✝️ remains an aid under Scripture, not a substitute for the ordinary means God gives through His Word and church.
