Citing NTW✝️

Use this page for proper citation, attribution, and reference guidance for NTW✝️.

If you quote or summarize a Navigate The Way ✝️ response in academic, ministry, or public writing, cite the platform, model, session title, date, and share link as clearly as possible.

Quick answer: Treat NTW✝️ like a generative AI source: identify the generated session, name the platform, include the model if known, give the date, and provide a share URL when one exists. Do not cite AI output as if it were Scripture, a pastor, or a peer-reviewed authority.

Citation Checklist

  • Session title or prompt: Use the auto-generated title or a concise description of what was asked.
  • Platform and tool: Name ChatGPT/Navigate The Way ✝️ or the platform where the exchange occurred.
  • Model and date: Include the model if available and the date you generated or accessed the answer.
  • Share link: Add a public share URL when the platform provides one.
  • Verification note: For theology or Scripture, make clear that important claims were checked against primary sources.

Example Formats

MLA Works Cited Example

“Troubled Jesus' Compassionate Response” auto-generated session title. ChatGPT/Navigate The Way ✝️, GPT-4o, OpenAI, 8 June 2024, https://chatgpt.com/share/7d0c171a-dbd8-4e17-88b5-efde639fa6e1.

MLA In-Text Example

According to Navigate The Way ✝️, “[quoted or paraphrased response text]” (“Troubled Jesus' Compassionate Response”).

APA Reference Example

Navigate The Way ✝️. (2024, June 8). Troubled Jesus' compassionate response [Auto-generated session title]. ChatGPT/Navigate The Way ✝️, GPT-4o, OpenAI. https://chatgpt.com/share/7d0c171a-dbd8-4e17-88b5-efde639fa6e1

APA In-Text Example

Navigate The Way ✝️ (2024) states, “[quoted or paraphrased response text]” (“Troubled Jesus' compassionate response”).

Do Not Overstate AI Authority

AI-generated text can help document a study process, but Scripture remains the final authority for Christian faith and practice. For published theological claims, cite primary biblical texts and reliable human sources rather than relying on AI output alone.
