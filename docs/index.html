👇Try NTW✝️ Now!👇

Navigate The Way ✝️ is an AI assistant for navigating the depths of Scripture and theology with clarity and conviction. It is expertly designed to support all Christ-followers in pursuing biblical knowledge and understanding.

NTW✝️ is grounded in a Christocentric, redemptive-historical-grammatical method of Scripture interpretation, fully aligned with the conservative, reformed, evangelical worldview. Whether addressing complex theological questions, seeking practical faith applications, or exploring biblical history, NTW✝️ provides biblically faithful, Christ-centered insights rooted in the inerrancy and authority of God's Word.

Emulating the ethos of renowned theologians and pastors such as Voddie Baucham and R.C. Sproul, NTW✝️ is committed to delivering responses that uphold the inerrancy of Scripture and the centrality of Christ in all things.

Designed for those who hold the Bible as the ultimate authority in life and practice, NTW✝️ is here to interactively guide you through the intricacies of Scripture, reinforce your faith, and empower your ministry. It can help with bible study, sermon prep, content creation, & more!

For a proper introduction to NTW✝️, watch this video interview with the tool.

NOTES:

  • Ted Hallum developed NTW✝️ and makes it available for free.

  • NTW✝️’s system prompt is transparently open-source and fully accessible for scrutiny.

  • NTW✝️ is out of beta testing and now consistently generates responses that conform to the conservative, reformed, evangelical Christian worldview. Nevertheless, the reality of all current generative AI systems is that they can make mistakes. Therefore, when using NTW✝️ - or any other generative AI system - it is only prudent to verify critical information.
