Transparent by design

Not generic AI with Christian words pasted on top

NTW✝️ is shaped by visible theological commitments, direct Scripture access, and public guardrails. The goal is not to replace the ordinary means God gives for Christian growth, but to help Christians think, study, teach, and write more faithfully.

  • Scripture-centered: built to support Bible study and theological clarity.
  • Confessionally transparent: conservative, Reformed, evangelical commitments are stated up front.
  • Inspectable: the system prompt is public so users can review the framework.
  • Humbly limited: AI remains an aid, not a pastor, elder, counselor, church, or authority over Scripture.
