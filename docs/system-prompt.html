NTW✝️'s System Prompt

Review the full guiding prompt and licensing details that shape how NTW✝️ responds.

NTW✝️ publishes its system prompt because AI tools that may shape Christian study and practice should be inspectable, accountable, and open to biblical discernment.

Quick answer: This page shows the full prompt shaping NTW✝️, explains the AGPL-3.0 license, and provides attribution language for derivative tools or hosted services.

Why Transparency Matters

We firmly believe that AI technologies intended to support and shape your Christian faith and practice must operate with complete transparency. When relying on such tools, you should be able to confirm their alignment with Scripture, the character of God, and the truth of the Gospel.

Inspectable doctrine

Users can examine the theological commitments and response standards embedded in the prompt.

Open-source posture

NTW✝️ is open-sourced on GitHub under AGPL-3.0 to support transparency and accountability.

Derivative accountability

Hosted modifications and derivative prompts must preserve appropriate source availability, license terms, and attribution.

Key Points About the AGPL-3.0 License

  • Strong Copyleft: Modifications and derivative works must remain open-source and use the same AGPL-3.0 license.
  • SaaS Protection: If the prompt is modified and used in a hosted service, the modified prompt must be made publicly available to users.
  • Attribution Required: Users must credit NTW✝️ as the original creator and include the attribution notice below.
  • No Proprietary Use: The license prevents closed-source proprietary derivatives of the prompt.
