Meet NTW✝️

Get introduced to Navigate The Way ✝️ and hear directly from its creator about mission, design, and use.

NTW✝️ is an AI-powered assistant created to support the spiritual growth and biblical understanding of its users. It operates under the conservative, reformed, evangelical perspective, focusing on the historical-grammatical method of scripture interpretation. This tool provides responses that align strictly with biblically accurate teachings, helping users navigate complex theological questions or deepen their scripture study.

Please become better acquainted with the tool by watching this video interview between NTW✝️ and its creator, Ted Hallum.
