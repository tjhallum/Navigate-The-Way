Meet NTW✝️

Get introduced to Navigate The Way ✝️ and hear directly from its creator about mission, design, and use.

Ted Hallum introduces Navigate The Way ✝️ by letting the tool speak for itself: what it is, how it works, how it handles Scripture, and how Christians can use it with discernment.

Watch NTW✝️ Introduce Itself

This short interview is the best starting point for new visitors. Ted asks NTW✝️ to explain its purpose, its historical-grammatical approach to Scripture, its ministry uses, and its posture toward curious, unsure, and skeptical Christians.

Watch on YouTube

What You’ll Hear

  • What NTW✝️ is: an assistant for church leaders, lay leaders, and followers of Christ seeking biblical and theological insight.
  • How it works: a plain-language explanation of generative AI as a conversational tool drawing on patterns from theological and biblical content.
  • How Scripture guides it: answers should be rooted in Scripture and handled in context rather than proof-texted to fit a preconceived point.
  • How it can help: Bible interpretation, theological questions, practical application, devotional content, teaching and preaching preparation, and difficult questions.
  • How to approach it: with curiosity, discernment, and healthy skepticism—always letting Scripture remain the authority.
